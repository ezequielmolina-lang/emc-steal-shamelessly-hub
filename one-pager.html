The Personalization Trap

AI tutors silently make three metacognitive decisions students should learn to make themselves: what to review, where the error is, and whether the approach works. Performance looks great while the AI is on. The question is what happens when it's removed.

AEA Registry: AEARCTR-0012847 • IRB Protocol 564

Control
Regular classes, no AI — ~1,320 students
Standard AI
Full AI tutor — ~2,640 students
Modified AI
AI + metacognitive scaffolding — ~2,640 students

Primary outcome: transfer test 4 months after removing the platform. If Modified AI wins, the trap is real — and has a solution.
